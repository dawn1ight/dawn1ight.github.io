首先解crypto1

hint1=x1∗p+y1∗q−0x11hint1=x1*p+y1*q-0x11hint1=x1∗p+y1∗q−0x11

hint2=x2∗p+y2∗q−0x514hint2=x2*p+y2*q-0x514hint2=x2∗p+y2∗q−0x514

hint1+0x114≡x1∗pmod  qhint1+0x114\equiv x1*p \mod qhint1+0x114≡x1∗pmodq

hint2+0x514≡x2∗pmod  qhint2+0x514\equiv x2*p \mod qhint2+0x514≡x2∗pmodq

令H1=hint1+0x1144,H2=hint2+0x514H1=hint1+0x1144,H2=hint2+0x514H1=hint1+0x1144,H2=hint2+0x514

x2H1≡x1H2mod  qx2H1\equiv x1H2 \mod qx2H1≡x1H2modq

由于$$x1$$和$$x2$$很小，直接爆破

再解crypto2，开始在hint = pow(514*p - 114*q, n - p - q, n)卡住了，后面想到

$hint = (514p - 114q)^{n - p - q} \mod n $

其中n−p−q=φ(n)−1n-p-q=\varphi(n)-1n−p−q=φ(n)−1

xn−p−q≡xφ(n)−1≡x−1≡hintmod  nx^{n-p-q}\equiv x^{\varphi(n)-1}\equiv x^{-1}\equiv hint \mod nxn−p−q≡xφ(n)−1≡x−1≡hintmodn

通过计算模逆元B并构建方程组求解

n=p∗q514p−114q=hint−1mod  nn=p*q\\514p-114q=hint^{-1}\mod nn=p∗q514p−114q=hint−1modn
